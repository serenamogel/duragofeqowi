GoFundMe: Ben Nothstine Obituary &amp; Funeral, Professional Realtor

Publish date: 2024-02-11

Unexpectedly Ben Nothstine passed away, leaving friends, relatives, and the entire community grieving and in shock.

While I’ve searched, to find the right words… I don’t think that there really are any words that will make it better. Our industry our community, and his amazing family are all now left with a hole that is just filled with grief, shock, and sadness. Benjamin Nothstine was and will continue to be an exemplary model for someone with a true, loving, caring, genuine heart. I had the pleasure of working with him and while it was only for a short time he made a lasting impression. The first day that I met Ben I told him he reminded me of my older brother. From his mannerisms to his passion in debating any topic, and the way that he would always ask what he could do for you before doing for himself! I think what will be missed the most is his sincere desire to leave any room and conversation happier than when he walked in! Every single time we would hang up the phone or I would just walk to my office down the hall, you would hear him tell anyone love you mean it. To his wife and his amazing beautiful daughter. I hope that you find peace in the coming days and know that you have an entire community rallying for you with you and behind you.
