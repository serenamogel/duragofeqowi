Jaya Bhattacharya is a household name in the Indian cinema and entertainment industry. She doesn’t need a detailed introduction to those who are already familiar with her and her top-notched acting projects. She is such a talented actress, be it television series or movies, she has proved herself an amazing star in the industry.

Career

Jaya Bhattacharya is popularly known for being a part of the longest-running television serial named ‘Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahoo Thi’. And among films, she is notable for portraying the role of Priya Gill’s best friend in the romantic love movie ‘Sirf Tum’ starring Sanjay Kapoor and Sushmita Sen.

Among others, her notable role is Jigyasa Bali in ‘Kasamh Se’, ‘Jhansi Ki Rani’. ‘Thapki Pyaar Ki’, and many more that are prominent on the list.

Jaya Bhattacharya is famous for her incredibly amazing acting chops and superb expressions. She has been playing supportive and leading roles as well.

Moreover, she is good at performing negative roles, but Bhattacharya is even great at everything, be it protagonist or antagonist roles.

Achievements

Jaya Bhattacharya is the recipient of several nominees and awards, but she calls fame, respect, and honor the greatest life achievement.
