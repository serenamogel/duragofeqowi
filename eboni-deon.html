Eboni Deon

Publish date: 2024-09-11

Eboni Deon works as an on-camera meteorologist for WISH-TV in Indianapolis. She was born in the city of Indianapolis, Indiana, in the United States.

Where was Eboni Deon born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

She began working as a weekend meteorologist for WFTV in June 2010. Eboni grew up in the Midwest, where the seasons changed dramatically.

She had a fascination with the weather since she was a child. Eboni has recently found solace in the heat and warmth of the south. She had a good upbringing in her birthplace, thanks to her gracious family.

Deon was a gifted student who graduated with honors from North Center High School in Indianapolis. After graduating from high school, she attended Jackson State University and earned a Bachelor of Science in Meteorology. Eboni later earned a master’s degree in geosciences from Mississippi State University, with a focus on broadcast meteorology.

Is Eboni Deon Married? Relationship

She is a married woman with two children. In her spare time, Eboni Deon enjoys traveling, dancing, and spending time with her family, friends, and two dogs, Bradley and Mister.

How did Eboni Deon start her Professional Career?

Deon started her career at the National Weather Network in Jackson, Mississippi. She then worked for The Weather Channel as a Radio Broadcast Meteorologist before becoming an on-camera meteorologist.

Eboni has also reported for CNN International on global weather issues, such as the devastating earthquake in Haiti and the Icelandic volcano, which disrupted air travel across Europe for days. She is also a member of the American Meteorological Society.

Deon started weather reporting for Al Jazeera America on weekends in 2013. She also began as a part-time employee in the mornings while chief meteorologist Nichole Mitchell was on reserve duty.
