Obituary information for Ohio resident Shannon Wingfield due to a tragic traffic accident

On the fateful morning of November 15, 2023, a tragic accident occurred on the I-70 highway near Newark, Licking County, Ohio, USA. It was a terrifying multi-vehicle collision, including a bus carrying students and teachers from Tuscarawas Valley Middle School.

According to witnesses, the accident occurred around 8:50 a.m. when a bus full of students was traveling in the right lane west of I-70. Suddenly, a cargo truck crashed into the back of the bus at high speed. The violent collision caused both vehicles to be seriously damaged, leak fuel and burst into flames.

The initial cause of the accident on Interstate 70

According to initial information from the Ohio State Highway Patrol, the cause of this tragic accident may have been due to the truck driver losing control of the vehicle’s speed, causing consecutive collisions with the rear of the bus, resulting in it losing control and striking several other vehicles. However, the specific cause is still under investigation for further clarification.

The National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) has deployed an experienced team of investigators to the scene to collaborate with local law enforcement. NTSB Chair Jennifer Homendy stated that experts will concentrate on gathering and analyzing data from the involved vehicles’ dash cameras, as well as other physical evidence, to shed light on the sequence of events.

The investigative team anticipates being on-site for 5-7 days to meticulously examine and gather evidence. Subsequently, a preliminary report will be released in a few weeks to provide initial insights into the accident’s cause.
