Top 10 WWE RAW Moments, Cesaro Reacts To His Teeth Being Fixed

Publish date: 2024-03-07

Top 10 WWE RAW Moments

Following on from another hectic edition of Monday Night RAW, WWE has put together a list of their top 10 moments from the show. The red brand is going full steam ahead towards Clash of Champions in just a few short weeks time, which has led to some intriguing booking decisions.

Which, of course, includes some great King of the Ring matches.

Cesaro Reacts To His Teeth Being Fixed

A few years ago, an in-ring injury caused Cesaro to chip his front teeth – leading him to wear braces and mouthguards ever since. Now, the former United States Champion has unveiled his brand new gnashers which look as good as new.

This comes ahead of Cesaro‘s appearance at NXT UK TakeOver: Cardiff this weekend, with it being revealed earlier that he would be in attendance to take a ‘hands-on’ look at the product.
