Who Is Hitman Hollas Girlfriend Cinnamon?

Publish date: 2024-05-15

Cinnamon, Hitman’s girlfriend, is a businesswoman and social media influencer.

Cinnamon owns and operates the CINNY online shopping and retail store, and she describes herself as the company’s CEO.

According to her Instagram bio, she travels between St. Louis and Atlanta. Cinnamon also runs a catering company named Cinny’s Food.

The same’s Instagram account features several of her dishes as well as a menu from which customers can order.

Cinnamon’s primary Instagram page, which has over 267,000 followers, features various photos of her rapper lover.

A ring emoticon appears next to her boyfriend’s name in her bio. It’s unclear whether they’re engaged or married.
