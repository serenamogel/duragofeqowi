Biography

Paris Whitney Hilton, born on February 17, 1981, in New York City, USA, is an American media personality.

She has a notable family connection as the great-granddaughter of Conrad Hilton, the founder of Hilton Hotels.

Paris began her journey as a model and later gained widespread recognition through her reality TV stint in “The Simple Life,” which aired from 2003 to 2007.

Beyond reality TV, Paris has ventured into movies and television shows, launched a music career, and embarked on various business endeavors.
