Adeel Hussain has very handsome and decent features in his personality with curly hair, a positive attitude and accomplished acting skills. He was initially a Vj and Rj who became a talented director, model, and actor. Adeel also has the fond of drawing, painting, and photography. He was born on 30th June 1987 in Karachi. He was the son of a Navy officer and is elders one among his siblings. Adeel got his early education from Karachi, where completed his O’ levels and A’ Levels. He got his Bachelor’s degree in Management Information System from Karachi. Late on he was sent to Ireland for the pursuit of higher education, where he got his Master’s degree in Marketing from the Dublin Business School, Ireland.

Career Journey

Adeel started his career journey as a video jockey (Vj). At the same time, due to his sweet voice, he got an opportunity to host a radio show City FM’s breakfast on the radio Pakistan in the 1990s. Later on, he turned himself towards the showbiz industry after accepting the advertisement offers. Now he started to work in many music videos, drama serials, and telefilms, such as Duniya Goal Hai which was played in the Kara Film Festival and the first acting performance of him in the drama industry. Till to date, he has worked in many memorable serials. He portrayed the leading character of Junaid in the drama serial “Daam” stirred with Sanam Baloch and Aamina Sheikh in 2010. In 2013 he made his debut in the most-watched drama of the year “’Mata-e-Jaan” as Ibad Uzair which was based on an Urdu novel which tells a story of the lives of two Columbian students, written by Farhat Ishtiaq and directed by Mehreen Jabbar.

He has also portrayed his film debut in a romantic film “Ho Mann Jahaan” in 2016 along with Mahira Khan and Sheheryar Munawar which was directed by Asim Raza and produced by Sheheryar Munawar. This film was considered a box office super hit film and collected 22.50 crores all around the world. Adeel gives his excellent performance as a special appearance in a hit film named ‘Bin Roye’ in 2015. he has also appeared in the Lollywood movie “Ho Mann Jahan”.

He also worked as a cinematographer in a documentary that was produced by Vizor Studios.

Achievements

Adeel has receipt six famous awards and nominated for various national awards until now. He was the recipients of Kara Film Festival Award in 2007 for special jury award for acting in Dunya Goal Hai”, Pakistan Media Award in 2009 for the best new male actor for acting in “Daam”, Galaxy Lollywood Award twice in 2016 for the beast special appearance in “Bin Raye” and in 2017 for the best dance performance in “Ho Man Jahan”, Nigar award for the best debut male and the Pakistan Excellent Award for the best actor in the same year and same movie.
